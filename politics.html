Political System

The Republic of the Walnut Islands™ is a sovereign nation-state. We are governed by a council of seven chairmen, each elected by the residents of each island (residents are required to pay a $425.00 USD representational fee in order to vote). All decisions made by the council must be approved by the Walnut Inc. board of directors before being implemented. Furthermore, our residents are free from income taxes and corporate taxes. Our system ensures the liberty and prosperity of our privelidged citizens.

Flag of the Walnut Islands

Law and Punishment

We expect our residents to follow our Code of Conduct. Rules include:

  1. no violence
  2. no profanity
  3. no possession of banned literature
  4. no slander
  5. no promotion of socialist or communist ideology

Failure to abide by these rules may result in fines, or, for more serious offenses, banishment.
